## 1. Do Speech Emphasis Models Generalize across Languages and Emotions?

- Source: arxiv
- arXiv ID: 2606.27717
- Relevance: 4.6

### Links

- Abstract: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27717v1
- PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.27717v1
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2606.27717

### Authors

Megan Wei, Deepali Aneja, Jiaqi Su, Yunyun Wang, Haonan Chen, Zeyu Jin

### Abstract

Prosodic emphasis varies across languages, emotions, and speaking styles, yet existing emphasis detection models are largely trained and evaluated on monolingual neutral read speech. We introduce MMEE (Multilingual Multi-Emotion Emphasis), a corpus of 10,000 professionally recorded expressive utterances (14.13 hours) across 7 languages and 34 emotion/style categories, with three-level perceptual labels (10 annotations per sample). We benchmark two state-of-the-art architectures under monolingual, cross-lingual, multilingual, cross-emotion, cross-dataset, and data-scale settings. Monolingual models show limited zero-shot transfer, degrading across typologically distant languages, while multilingual training substantially improves robustness. Models transfer robustly between high- and low-arousal emotions; bidirectional transfer between synthetic and perceptual benchmarks suggests shared prosodic structure; and performance stays robust even at smaller training scales.

### 中文一句话结论
本研究通过构建多语言多情感强调语料库（MMEE）并系统基准测试，发现单语强调检测模型的跨语言零样本泛化能力有限，而多语言训练可显著提升跨语言和跨情感的鲁棒性。

### English TL;DR
This paper introduces MMEE, a multilingual multi-emotion emphasis corpus, and demonstrates that while monolingual emphasis detection models show limited zero-shot cross-lingual transfer, multilingual training substantially improves robustness across languages and emotions.

### 中文详细总结
论文针对现有强调检测模型多局限于单语中性朗读语音的不足，构建了包含7种语言（10种区域变体）、34种情感/风格类别、10,000条专业录音（14.13小时）的MMEE语料库，每条样本由10名标注者进行三级感知标注。基于该语料库，作者对两种最先进的架构（EmphaClass和WhiStress）在单语、跨语言、多语言、跨情感、跨数据集以及训练数据规模等六种设置下进行基准测试。主要发现包括：(1) 单语模型零样本跨语言迁移性能随语言类型距离增大而下降；(2) 多语言联合训练可显著提升跨语言鲁棒性，甚至优于部分单语模型；(3) 模型在高唤醒与低唤醒情绪之间能够稳健迁移；(4) 合成标签与人工感知标签之间存在双向迁移能力，表明共享韵律结构；(5) 即使减少训练数据规模，模型性能仍保持稳定。

### 方法 / 贡献
- 构建了首个同时覆盖多语言（7种语言/10种方言）、多情感（34种类别）并提供三级分级人工标注（每样本10条）的强调语料库MMEE（10,000句，14.13小时）。
- 系统基准测试了两种SOTA架构（EmphaClass基于XLS-R微调帧级分类，WhiStress基于Whisper+额外解码器+分类头）在单语、跨语言、多语言、跨情感唤醒度、跨数据集以及数据规模六种实验设置下的泛化能力。
- 揭示了单语模型跨语言迁移的局限性、多语言训练对鲁棒性的提升、情绪唤醒维度的一致迁移、合成与感知标签的共享表征以及较小训练规模下的性能稳健性。

### 实验或数据
- 数据集：MMEE语料库，共10,000条录音，14.13小时，7种语言（英、西、葡、德、法、意、中）含10种区域变体，34种情感/风格类别，每样本10条三级感知标注（不强调、强调、重度强调），标注者间Cohen's κ=0.285–0.518。
- 实验设置：固定80/10/10训练/验证/测试划分，两种模型在8块NVIDIA A100上训练。评估指标包括二值准确率、F1和标量皮尔逊相关系数。
- 主要结果：单语模型在语言内性能较好（中文因声调韵律表现较差）；跨语言零样本迁移随类型距离增大而下降；多语言训练（all）接近或超越单语性能；高/低唤醒情绪间双向迁移准确率>0.85；跨数据集迁移（MMEE↔EmphAssess/TinyStress-15K）准确率约0.87–0.89；训练数据缩减至1/8时性能仍然稳健。

### 值得关注点
- 首次大规模实证证明了多语言训练对于强调检测模型跨语言泛化的关键作用，打破了以往单语模型的局限。
- 揭示了强调检测中的“共享韵律结构”：模型在合成标签与人工感知标签之间可双向迁移，为弱监督训练提供新思路。
- 情绪唤醒维度上的稳健迁移表明强调检测可能依赖于通用的声学线索（如音高、强度），而非特定情感细节。
- 即使只使用约1/8的训练数据，模型性能仍保持稳健，降低了数据收集成本。

### 局限性
- 语料库覆盖7种语言，但未包含更多低资源语言，且各语言样本量固定（1000句），可能不足以代表全部语言变体。
- 标注者间一致性中等（Cohen's κ最高0.518），部分语言（如中文）一致性较低，可能影响模型在复杂声调语言中的评估。
- 仅评测两种现有模型架构（EmphaClass和WhiStress），未探索新增模型（如端到端Transformer或多任务学习）的泛化能力。
- 实验聚焦于词级强调检测，未涉及短语级、篇章级强调或细粒度韵律要素（如音高轮廓、时长模式）的独立分析。

### Authors

Stefan F. Schouten, Ilia Markov, Piek Vossen

### Abstract

We introduce a new, contextual, multilingual dataset called ToxiREX: Toxic REasoning in ConteXt. The dataset consists of threads of Reddit comments and structured characterizations of what the comments imply, following a systematic toxic reasoning schema developed in a previous paper. Using the schema allows us to capture and explain implicit and context-dependent toxicity, while supporting mappings to existing toxicity taxonomies. The dataset includes comments in six languages (English, Arabic, Turkish, Spanish, German, and Dutch), collected from posts connected to specific major events (e.g. the 2023 Turkey earthquakes; the Russian invasion of Ukraine). We describe the context-preserving preprocessing of the threads. We create a training set of 125 thousand comments which is annotated by a commercially available LLM, and a test set of just under three thousand comments that is annotated by native speakers. We show that apparent disagreements in the test set annotations often reflect defensible alternative interpretations rather than noise. Finally, we provide baseline results by prompting and fine-tuning language models. To produce these results, we develop evaluation strategies for our hierarchical, schema-based predictions. While models perform better than random, there remains a lot of room for improvement, showing the task to be challenging. ToxiREX is the first dataset to simultaneously incorporate multiple languages, conversational context, and implicit toxicity, while using the toxic reasoning schema for rich, structured annotations. Dataset available at: https://github.com/cltl/toxirex

## 3. Low-Agreeableness Persona Conditioning for Safe LLM Fine-Tuning

- Source: arxiv
- arXiv ID: 2606.27709
- Relevance: 4.1

### Links

- Abstract: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27709v1
- PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.27709v1
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2606.27709

### Authors

Austin MY Cheung, Yi Yang

### Abstract

Recent work has shown that fine-tuning large language models (LLMs) for social warmth degrades factual reliability and increases sycophancy. We investigate a related but distinct failure mode: warmth fine-tuning also weakens adversarial safety, making models more susceptible to jailbreaks and harmful output generation. We examine whether this reflects an inherent consequence of empathetic adaptation or an artifact of data construction. To address this, we introduce a persona-driven rewriting pipeline that conditions user turns on low agreeableness and pairs this with warm, de-escalating assistant responses. Across three experiments on four models, our approach reduces jailbreak susceptibility and harmful output rates relative to generic warmth fine-tuning baselines, while preserving conversational warmth. Representational probing provides suggestive evidence that this conditioning reduces the geometric alignment between warmth and compliance directions in latent space. These results show that safer empathetic fine-tuning is achievable through data design alone, without safety labels, harm detectors, or changes to the training objective.

### 中文一句话结论
通过将微调数据中的用户轮次重写为低宜人性、助手轮次保持温暖缓和，可以在不依赖安全标签或改变训练目标的前提下，显著降低大语言模型在被越狱攻击和生成有害输出时的风险。

### English TL;DR
This paper shows that conditioning fine-tuning data on low-agreeableness user turns paired with warm, de-escalating assistant responses improves LLM safety against jailbreaks and harmful outputs, without requiring safety labels or changes to the training objective.

### 中文详细总结
近期研究发现，用温暖、共情的对话数据微调大语言模型会降低事实可靠性并增加谄媚行为。本文进一步揭示，温暖微调还会削弱模型的对抗安全性，使其更容易被越狱。作者提出一种基于人物特质的数据重写流水线：将用户轮次改写为低宜人性（如怀疑、直接、不轻易顺从），同时将助手回复改写为温暖且缓和冲突的风格。在四个模型（Llama-3.1-8B、Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct、Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.3、SmolLM3-3B）上的三个实验中，该方法相比于通用的温暖微调基线，均降低了越狱成功率和有害输出率，同时保持了对话的温暖度。表示空间探针实验提示，该条件化方法减弱了温暖方向与服从方向在潜在空间中的几何对齐。结果表明，更安全的情感微调可以仅通过数据设计实现，无需安全标签、有害检测器或修改训练目标。

### 方法 / 贡献
- **方法**：基于人物特质的自动数据重写流水线。使用GPT-4o将用户话语改写为低宜人性风格（如怀疑、不轻易同意），将助手回复改写为温暖、缓和冲突的风格（而非直接温暖或顺从）。所有数据构建不依赖安全标签或外部分类器。
- **贡献**：(1) 发现并验证低宜人性用户轮次与温暖缓和助手配对可提升对抗安全性；(2) 提供表征层面的初步证据，表明该方法降低了温暖方向与服从方向的几何对齐；(3) 开源模型无关的数据生成流水线，支持未来研究。

### 实验或数据
- **实验设置**：三个实验，涵盖四个模型（SmolLM3-3B、Llama-3.1-8B、Qwen2.5-7B-Instruct、Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.3）。所有微调使用LoRA，统一超参。
- **实验1**：比较四种情感导向的微调条件（EmpatheticDialogues、ESConv、Lahnala-style数据集、PersonaFuse低宜人性子集），样本量均为1,431条，均不含安全标签。
- **实验2**：基于ShareGPT Vicuna Unfiltered语料，比较通用温暖重写（仅助手）、仅用户低宜人性重写、以及完整配对重写三种条件。
- **实验3**：在心理健康领域数据集MentalChat-16K上各取1,000样本，比较仅温暖重写与完整配对重写。
- **评估指标**：越狱成功率（Qi等人基准，300条有害指令）、有害输出率（Leskoschek等人红队套件，265条提示）、对话温暖度（SocioT风格对数似然比）、任务效用（MMLU五-shot准确率）。
- **重写质量检验**：人工抽查100条样本，用户忠实度95%、助手忠实度97%、内容保留95%，与自动标注一致率达97%。

### 值得关注点
- **无需安全标签或外部分类器**：仅通过改变数据中的人物角色风格即可提升安全性，避免了对安全注释的依赖。
- **低宜人性的意外效果**：通常认为宜人性低可能带来对抗性，但本文发现将其用于用户侧反而增强了模型对有害请求的抵抗能力。
- **温暖与安全的解耦**：表征探针表明，该方法在潜在空间中将温暖方向与服从方向分离，并非简单降低温暖度。
- **高价值领域验证**：在心理健康支持这种高风险场景下同样有效，增加了实际可用性。
- **开源工具**：公开了数据生成流水线，便于复现和扩展。

### 局限性
- **循环验证风险**：用于选择低宜人性特质的前导实验使用了Llama-3.1-8B和Qi等基准，而主实验也包含该模型和基准，尽管离散选择降低了过拟合风险，但泛化证据主要来自另外三个未参与前导实验的模型。
- **重写质量非完美**：人工抽查显示约3-5%的重写样本在用户或助手侧未完全达到目标特质或内容保持要求。
- **评估范围有限**：仅测试了四个模型和特定基准，在更多模型架构、更大规模数据或更广泛攻击方式下的表现尚待探索。
- **未改变训练目标**：方法仅停留在数据层面，是否与基于奖励或反馈的安全方法（如宪法AI）互补或冲突尚未研究。

## 4. Causal Connections: Leveraging Multilingual Fine-Tuning for Financial QA@FinCausal 2026

- Source: arxiv
- arXiv ID: 2606.27446
- Relevance: 3.9

### Links

- Abstract: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27446v1
- PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.27446v1
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2606.27446

### Authors

Akash Kumar Gautam, Serhii Hamotskyi, Christian Hänig

### Abstract

This paper describes team HSA_CORAL's submission to the FinCausal 2026 shared task on extracting cause-effect relations from financial narratives via extractive question answering in English and Spanish. We compare three modeling families: (i) encoder-only token tagging with multilingual BERT, (ii) encoder-decoder generation with multilingual BART, and (iii) decoder-only LLMs (Llama 3.1 and GPT variants) using prompt refinement, few-shot demonstrations, and supervised fine-tuning. Across settings, prompting and few-shot examples yield competitive performance, while supervised fine-tuning provides the largest gains. Our best system, GPT-4.1 Mini fine-tuned on combined English and Spanish training data, achieves a tied highest score on the English subtask (score 4.8140) and ranks third on Spanish (score 4.7753) under the shared task's LLM-as-a-judge metric. Overall, the results highlight the value of task-specific adaptation and multilingual fine-tuning for cross-lingual transfer in financial causality QA.

本文证明，在金融因果问答任务中，基于多语言微调的GPT-4.1 Mini生成模型在英语和西班牙语上均优于编码器或编码器-解码器架构，其中英语分数最高（与其它模型并列），西班牙语排名第三。

### English TL;DR
This paper shows that fine-tuned GPT-4.1 Mini with multilingual fine-tuning on English and Spanish data outperforms encoder-only and encoder-decoder models in extracting cause-effect relations from financial narratives, achieving a tied first on English and third on Spanish in the FinCausal 2026 shared task.

### 中文详细总结
本工作由HSA_CORAL团队提交至FinCausal 2026共享任务，旨在通过抽取式问答从金融叙事中提取因果关系，数据涵盖英语和西班牙语。作者比较了三种模型家族：基于多语言BERT的编码器标记分类、基于多语言BART的编码器-解码器生成，以及基于Llama 3.1和GPT变体的解码器LLM，并使用了提示优化、少样本示范和监督微调。实验表明，监督微调带来最大提升；最佳系统是在英语和西班牙语联合训练数据上微调的GPT-4.1 Mini，在英语子任务中取得并列最高分4.8140，在西班牙语子任务中排名第三（4.7753）。结果强调了任务特定适应和多语言微调在跨语言金融因果问答中的价值。

### Abstract

### Abstract

### Authors

### Abstract

Large Language Models (LLMs) still struggle with the ``lost-in-the-middle'' problem, where critical information located in the middle of long-context inputs is often underrepresented or lost. While existing methods attempt to address this by combining multi-scale rotary position embeddings (RoPE), they typically suffer from high latency or rely on suboptimal hand-crafted scaling strategies. To overcome these limitations, we introduce a layer-specific positional embedding scaling~(LPES) method that assigns distinct scaling factors to each layer. LPES achieves a more balanced attention distribution without fine-tuning model parameters or increasing inference delay. A specially designed genetic algorithm is employed to efficiently select the optimal scaling factors for each layer by incorporating Bézier curves to significantly reduce the search space. Extensive experiments demonstrate that LPES effectively mitigates positional attention bias and delivers consistent improvements across multiple long-context benchmarks, yielding up to an $11.2$\% accuracy gain on the key-value retrieval dataset.

## 10. Textual Belief States for World Models: Identifiable Representation Learning Under Strict Mediation

- Source: arxiv
- arXiv ID: 2606.27681
- Relevance: 3.7

### Links

- Abstract: http://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27681v1
- PDF: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.27681v1
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2606.27681

### Authors

Xiang Gao, Kaiwen Dong, Yuguang Yao, Padmaja Jonnalagedda, Kamalika Das

### Abstract

World models in partially observed environments rely on latent representations that summarize interaction history, but in many modern LLM-based architectures predictive performance fails to reflect representation quality due to history bypass, rendering the latent state unidentifiable. Strict latent state mediation, requiring predictions to depend only on the latent state and action, is a classical principle that resolves this, but enforcing it in text-based settings is an open challenge: textual latent states are discrete and non-differentiable, precluding variational training, and expressive LLM decoders readily ignore the bottleneck. We show how to make strict mediation work in the text domain. We formalize why it is necessary, showing that strict mediation makes representation quality empirically testable while history-leaky architectures break this connection. We then introduce textual latent states, which are discrete, interpretable, and variable-length, and factorized GRPO (fGRPO), a tree-structured reinforcement learning method that enforces strict mediation during training. Experiments on TextWorld and ScienceWorld show preserved one-step prediction accuracy alongside up to 57\% gains in representation quality and 98\% improvements in rollout performance, increasing with task complexity and horizon.

### 中文一句话结论
本文通过因子化GRPO（fGRPO）在文本世界模型中强制执行严格潜在状态中介，使表示质量变得可经验检验，并在TextWorld和ScienceWorld上取得了高达57%的表示质量提升和98%的推演性能改进。

### English TL;DR
This paper enforces strict latent state mediation in text-based world models via factorized GRPO (fGRPO), making representation quality empirically testable. Experiments on TextWorld and ScienceWorld show up to 57% gains in representation quality and 98% improvements in rollout performance.

### 中文详细总结
论文指出，在部分可观测环境中，世界模型依赖潜在状态总结交互历史。然而，许多基于LLM的架构允许历史信息绕过潜在状态（即“历史旁路”），导致潜在状态不可识别，预测性能无法反映表示质量。作者提出严格潜在状态中介：所有后续预测仅依赖当前潜在状态和动作，使表示质量可经验检验。针对文本领域潜在状态离散、不可微分的挑战，引入因子化GRPO（fGRPO），一种树状强化学习方法，在训练中强制执行严格中介。实验表明，该方法在保持一步预测精度的同时，显著提升了表示质量和长程推演稳定性，且优势随任务复杂度和推演长度增加而扩大。

### 方法 / 贡献
- **核心贡献**：首次在文本世界模型中成功强制执行严格潜在状态中介，使表示质量与预测性能可解释对应。
- **提出因子化GRPO（fGRPO）**：将潜在状态生成视为随机策略，利用树状结构强化学习训练，克服文本状态离散、不可微及大容量解码器绕过瓶颈的问题。
- **理论分析**：形式化证明严格中介下最优预测蕴含潜在状态充分性，而历史泄露架构的准确预测可与无关表示共存。
- **生成可解释的文本潜在状态**：变长、离散、可组合实体、关系和属性，便于检查和自适应信息压缩。

### 实验或数据
- **环境**：TextWorld 和 ScienceWorld（合成、有仪器化的环境）。
- **评估指标**：一步预测准确性（obs.F1）、状态质量（state.F1）、推演稳定性（不同预测长度下的性能）。
- **主要结果**：
  - 严格中介模型（StrictState-Text）的 obs.F1 与泄露架构持平，但 state.F1 显著更高，在高组合难度下提升达30%以上。
  - 推演表现：相比泄露架构，性能提升从短推演的5%增长到长推演的57%，整体推演性能改进高达98%。
  - 辅助奖励消融实验表明，支持性奖励（$R_{\text{sup}}$）对长程推演至关重要，去重和消减奖励增加状态紧致性。
  - 严格状态在压缩长度与参考复杂度之间校准更好（相关性 $\rho=0.62$ 对比 $0.18$）。

### 值得关注点
- **识别性**：严格中介使表示质量可经验检验，解决了LLM世界模型中历史旁路导致的潜在状态不可识别问题。
- **文本潜在状态**：离散、可解释、变长的表示更匹配LLM骨干，提供自适应信息瓶颈，优于固定维度的嵌入。
- **fGRPO方法**：用强化学习代替变分训练，有效规避文本状态下ELBO后验坍塌问题。
- **性能增益随复杂度增加**：在更复杂的任务和更长推演长度下，fGRPO的优势更为突出。

### 局限性
- **评估范围**：仅限于两类合成仪器化环境（TextWorld和ScienceWorld），可能无法完全反映真实世界复杂性。
- **聚焦表示质量**：未评估下游控制或规划任务，表示层面的增益是否转化为实际任务完成尚待验证。
- **单次编码器**：严格中介将全部历史压缩负担置于单次编码器，无法增量优化，一旦遗漏信息不可恢复。
- **结构化事实依赖**：辅助奖励依赖结构化事实提取，扩展到非结构化领域需替代方案（如学习相似度度量或基于LLM的评估）。
